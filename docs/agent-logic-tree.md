flowchart TD
    START((Negotiation entry · counter below loadboard)):::entry
    SIGNALS[Read signals<br/>sentiment · pickup urgency]:::action
    ROUTE{Route by sentiment<br/>+ urgency}:::decision
    STRAT_WARM[Warm · mirror · label feelings<br/>tactical-empathy posture]:::action
    STRAT_FIRM[Firm · scarcity · market-anchored<br/>confident posture]:::action
    STRAT_BAL[Balanced · fact-based<br/>lane-average anchored]:::action
    BAND[Pick specific dollar in round band<br/>R1 closer to loadboard · R3 = floor]:::action
    FLOOR{Counter ≥ floor?}:::decision
    RAISE[Raise to floor]:::action
    READY[Counter ready]:::action
    REPLY2{Carrier next move?}:::decision
    DEFLECT[Deflect floor-extraction<br/>pivot to their number]:::action
    EXIT_OK((→ Transfer · agreed rate locked)):::successEnd
    EXIT_NEXT((→ Try next matching load)):::declineEnd

    START --> SIGNALS
    SIGNALS --> ROUTE
    ROUTE -->|frustrated sentiment<br/>or HIGH pickup urgency| STRAT_WARM
    ROUTE -->|neutral / positive sentiment<br/>normal urgency| STRAT_FIRM
    ROUTE -->|signals mixed or ambiguous| STRAT_BAL
    STRAT_WARM --> BAND
    STRAT_FIRM --> BAND
    STRAT_BAL --> BAND
    BAND --> FLOOR
    FLOOR -->|no| RAISE
    RAISE --> READY
    FLOOR -->|yes| READY
    READY --> REPLY2
    REPLY2 -->|accepts| EXIT_OK
    REPLY2 -->|new counter · next round<br/>if rounds left| SIGNALS
    REPLY2 -->|repeats same dollar| READY
    REPLY2 -->|floor-extraction question| DEFLECT
    DEFLECT --> READY
    REPLY2 -->|walks · or stalls after R3| EXIT_NEXT
